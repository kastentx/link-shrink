LinkShrink: For your URL shortening needs.

Type in the full URL of a website in the address bar above, after this page's URL.

The shortened URL that gets returned will now send you to your link!

Example Usage:

http://link-shrink.herokuapp.com/http://www.google.com http://link-shrink.herokuapp.com/http://www.freecodecamp.com

Example Output:

http://link-shrink.herokuapp.com/778468
